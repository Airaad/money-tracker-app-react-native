Privacy Policy

Last Update Date: July 4, 2025

Thank you for using Expensy. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

1. No Personal Information Collected

We do not collect or request any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or any other identifiable data.

2. Local-First Data Storage

All data you input in the app (e.g., expenses, categories) is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or sync your data to any remote servers.

3. No Internet Connectivity Required

The app is fully functional offline. It does not require internet access to work, and it does not send any data to us or any third parties.

4. Your Control

Because your data never leaves your device, you have full control. If you uninstall the app or clear its data, all your information will be permanently deleted.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with or rely on third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.
